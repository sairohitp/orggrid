Relationships are the backbone of private markets — private equity, venture capital, investment banking, and beyond. Every deal, every partnership, every exit traces back to who you know and how well you know them.

But the work of building and maintaining relationships gets derailed by busywork. The context, the connections, the follow-through. It's all there, scattered across the inboxes and calendars of your team. No one has time to piece it together.

That's why OrgGrid exists: to give you time back for the work that actually requires you.
